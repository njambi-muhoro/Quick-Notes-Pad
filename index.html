Click on the Write notes button to access the notes text area.
After writing your notes, click on the post button
and you will be able to see the notes you have written
When the notes appear, it will have a delete button
whereby, you can delete the notes if you no longer need it.
An update button will appear next to the delete button.
The update button will let you access that specific note for updates.

Once you post your notes or update your notes
refresh the page to remove the edit form
